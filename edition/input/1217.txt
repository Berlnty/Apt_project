                                                                                                                                                           <p>The Audi S5 is the high performance version of Audi’s A5 coupe, although the chassis is different. S5 has been in production since 2007, while production of the S5 Cabriolet began in 2009, with the all-wheel-drive version introduced in late 2016. </p>                                                                                                                                                                                <p>Redesigned for 2018, the S5 Cabriolet features an acoustic folding roof to help absorb unwanted noise, which also reduces the convertible’s weight by 88 pounds over the coupe.
