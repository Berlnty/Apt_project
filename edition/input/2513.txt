class="mw-editsection-divider"> | </span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noma&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Gyara sashe: NOMA">Gyara masomin</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2><p><a href="/wiki/Hausawa" title="Hausawa">Hausawa</a> Suka ce "Noma na duke tsohon ciniki, kowa yazo <a href="/wiki/Duniya" title="Duniya">duniya</a> kai ya taras"</p><p><a href="//commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Special:UploadWizard?uselang=ha&amp;wpDestFile=Field,_Corn,_Liechtenstein,_Mountains,_Alps,_Lanscape.jpg" class="new" title="File:Field, Corn, Liechtenstein, Mountains, Alps, Lanscape.jpg">File:Field, Corn, Liechtenstein, Mountains, Alps, Lanscape.jpg</a></p><ul><li>Menene Noma?</li></ul><p>Noma na da ma'anoni da dama kasancewar irin amfanin sa a fannoni daban-daban.</p><pre>To amma dai a fassara mafi sauki, zamu ce, Noma wata hanyar <a href="/wiki/Sana%27a" title="Sana&#39;a">sana'a</a> ce domin samun <a href="/wiki/Abinci" title="Abinci">abinci</a> da kudin shiga.Haka zalika noma wata babbar hanya ce ta samun karuwar <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tattalin_arziki&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tattalin arziki (babu wannan shafi)">tattalin arziki</a> da al'ummai suka raja'a akan ta. A <a href="/w/index.php?title=Kasar_hausa&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kasar hausa (babu wannan shafi)">kasar hausa</a> ko kuwa, noma shine babbar hanya ta samun haɓakar <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tattalin_arziki&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tattalin arziki (babu wannan shafi)">tattalin arziki</a>. Da noma ne <a href="/wiki/Hausawa" title="Hausawa">hausawa</a> suka fi samun kudin shiga da <a href="/wiki/Abinci" title="Abinci">abinci</a> dama kayayyakin da ake sarrafawa a <a href="/w/index.php?title=Masana%27anta&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Masana&#39;anta (babu wannan shafi)">masana'anta</a> kamar <a href="/w/index.php?title=Tufafi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Tufafi (babu wannan shafi)">tufafi</a> da sauran su.A wajen <a href="/wiki/Hausawa" title="Hausawa">hausawa</a> musamman mazauna <a href="/w/index.php?title=Karkara&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Karkara (babu wannan shafi)">karkara</a> noma ya zama kamar wajibi (dole) a wajen kowanne bahaushe musamman magidanci. Kusan muce ma ko wanne bahaushe ya iya noma yasan yarda ake yin shi.</pre><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="YARDA_AKE_NOMA">YARDA AKE NOMA</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span tayi, manoma rani zasu farane da share gonakin su, daga nan kuma sai su yi kaftu su ja kwamame sai su shuka abin da zasu shuka a cikin kwamamen.Suna yin amfani da injinan banruwa wajen bawa shukokin su ruwa har su girma su isa girbi.</pre><p>Akasarin abubuwan da ake nomawa a noman rani ana shuka su ne sabo da sayarwa domin samun kudin shiga.</p><p>Ga irin abubuwan da ake nomawa da
