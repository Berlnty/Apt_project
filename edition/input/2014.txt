To study this proposalfurther, two hypervariable segments of mtDNA were sequenced from 189people of diverse geographic origin, including 121 native Africans.Geographic specificity was observed in that identical mtDNA types areshared within but not between populations. A tree relating these mtDNAsequences to one another and to a chimpanzee sequence has many deepbranches leading exclusively to African mtDNAs. An African origin forhuman mtDNA is supported by two statistical tests. With the use of thechimpanzee and human sequences to calibrate the rate of mtDNA evolution,the age of the common human mtDNA ancestor is placed between 166,000 and249,000 years.
