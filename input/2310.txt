N'wansati vale Thayilandi</div></div></div><p><b>Vanhu</b>&#160;vamanguva lawa (<i>Homo sapiens</i>) hikuya hi <a href="/wiki/Sayensi" title="Sayensi">Sayensi</a>&#160;imuxaka wa rhavi ra swiharhi ra&#160;Hominina&#160;(Vanhu), leri riyelanaka na ntlawa wa timfenhe. Riviti ra Xilatini, "<i>homō"</i> rivula "munhu" kumbe "munhu wa laha misaveni"<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> naswona "<i>sapiens"</i>&#160; yivula "kuthlariha".<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup></p><p>Vanhu vahlawulekisiwa eka timfenhe hi mafambele na mayimele ya vona, kuhlanganisa na vuswikoti byo tirhisa mathulusi ku endla mintirho yo hambana hambana, naswona va na mabyongo lama kulukumba loko maringanisiwa na swiharhi.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></p><p>Swimunhwana swo sungula, hakanyingi a swi hlayiwi swiri vanhu —ngopfungopfu australopithecines, leswi a swiri namabyongo na kuakeka ka miri loku yelanaka na timfenhe.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>&#160;Swotala eka swimunhwana leswi aswiiswikota ku tirhisa ndzilo, naswona aswi ake no tshama eka tindhzawu totala ta tiko-nkulu ra Yuraxiya, naswona hiswona swi tswaleke muxaka wa vanhu va manguva lawa (<i>Homo sapiens) e</i> <a href="/wiki/Afrika" title="Afrika">Afrika</a>&#160;kwalomu ka 200,000 wa malembe lamahundzeke.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>&#160;Swisungule ku kombisa mahanyelo yohluvuka kwalomu ka&#160;50,000 wa malembe lamahundzeke. Vanhu vamanguva lawa vasungule ku valanga na tiko nkulu ra Afrika ivi va hangalaka na misava hinkwayo.<sup id="cite_ref-evolutionthe1st4billionyears_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-evolutionthe1st4billionyears-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></p><p>Kuhangalaka ka vanhu na ku andza ka nhlayo yavona &#160;kavanyete mbangu na swimilana kuhlanganisa na swiharhi switala emisaveni. Ku humelala ka vanhu ku suka eka mabyongo ya vona lamakulu lama endleke leswaku vaswikota ku anakanya mianakanyo leyi rharhanganeke, <a href="/wiki/Ririmi" title="Ririmi">tindzimi</a>, kuthlanthla swiphiqo, ku vumba rixaka na <a href="/wiki/Ndhzavuko" class="mw-redirect" title="Ndhzavuko">ndhzavuko</a> hi kuya hi ku ringisa van'wana. Vanhu vana switirhisiwa swotala leswi va switirhisaka kutlula swiharhi, naswona hi vona ntsena vakotaka ku tshivela ndzilo no sweka swakudya swa vona, nakambe hivona ntsena va ambalaka swi ambalo vathlela va tumbuluxa no tirhisa <a href="/wiki/Thekinoloji" title="Thekinoloji">thekinoloji</a> na <a href="/wiki/Vutshila" title="Vutshila">vutshila</a>. &#160;</p><p>Vanhu vana vuswikoti lebyi kulu byo tirhisa mimfungo ku hundzisa marungula (tani hi ririmi na vutshila) leswaku vatiphofula na avelana miehleketo, naswona vathlela va ti hlawula hi swiyenge leswi ngana xikongomela xokarhi. Vanhu va tumbuluxe swiyimiso swa mahanyele leswi rharhanganeke leswi akiwaka hi mintlawa leyi tirhisanaka kumbe leyi luthanaka, kusukela eka ndyangu, vuxaka na politiki.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>&#160; kunavela vutivi na ku navela kutwisisa na hlohlotela mbangu naswona vathlela va navela ku kongomisa swiendlakalo, swi tumbuluxe vutivi bya Sayensi, Filosofi, swiyilana na man'wana marhavi ya vutivi.</p><p>Hambi leswi vutomi bya vanhu vo tala abyi titshega eka vuhloti na mintlawa ya vavalangi,<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>&#160; nhlayo leyikulaka ya vanhu yi sungule to tihanyisa hi Vurimi na Swifuwo, kwalomu ka&#160;10,000 wa malembe lamahundzeke,<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>&#160;naswova leswi va endle leswaku va kota ku simeka nhluvuko. Mintlawa leyi ya va aka tiko yi yemahlweni yi ndlandlamuka leswi endleke leswaku yi simeka mfumo, vukhongeri na ndhzavuko emisaveni hinkwayo, hiku endla tano swi hlanganisa vanhu hi miganga leswaku va aka matiko na mimfumo. Nhluvuko lowu tisiweke hi ta sayensi na vutshunguri eka malembe-xidzana ya khume-nkaye (19) na makume-mbirhi (20), swi tise tithekinoloji ta mafurha ya mimovha na ku antswisiwa ka swa tarihanyo, leswi endleke nhlayo ya vanhu yikula hirivilo leri kulu.
