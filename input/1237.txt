spreadsheet?</h1>						<p><em>Foundation</em></p>						<p>A spreadsheet is a program used for performing calculations on tables of numbers. It can also often produce graphs and charts of this data. A spreadsheet is a grid of cells each with its own <strong>cell reference</strong>.</p>						<div class="bs-image bs-image-center" style="width:468px;">							<img src="/staticarchive/0da8e237e5b870dae941115f343147eaa4286b13.gif" width="468" height="151" alt="Example of a spreadsheet" title="Example of a spreadsheet with 4 columns"></div>						<p>Each cell can hold an item of data which is either <strong>text</strong> or a <strong>value</strong> or a <strong>formula</strong>. Cell data can come in text form or a value or as a formula.</p>					</div>					<div class="bs-pagination bs-pagination-bottom blq-clearfix">						<div class="bs-pages">							<div
